Financial Information

What is Finance?

Finance is the study and discipline of money, currency, and capital aspects. It also involves the management of money and includes activities such as investing, borrowing, lending, saving, and budgeting.

Why is Finance Required?

Finance is vital in any economic system as it allows individuals and companies to purchase products and services beyond their immediate financial reach. Financing helps leverage the time value of money by putting future expected income to use for current projects.

What are Investments?

An investment is an asset or item purchased with the expectation that it will generate income or appreciate in value in the future.

Example: Purchasing land for ₹1 crore with the hope that it can be sold for ₹2 crores in the future.

What is a Loan?

A loan is money, property, or other material goods given to another party in exchange for future repayment of the original amount along with interest.

Types of Loans

  • Home Loan
  • Vehicle Loan
  • Gold Loan
  • Secured Loan
  • Other Personal or Business Loans

Methods to Save Money

  • Avoid excessive debt, as it reduces savings.
  • Create a budget and track expenses regularly.
  • Prioritize paying off high-interest debts.
  • Build an emergency fund.
  • Use extra or unexpected income wisely.
  • Use credit cards responsibly.
